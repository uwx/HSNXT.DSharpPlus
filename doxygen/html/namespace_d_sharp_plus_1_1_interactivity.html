◆ StringSplittingLogic

enum DSharpPlus.Interactivity.StringSplittingLogic : byte
strong
Enumerator
Verbatim 

Cut every string at the length limit (slightly under 2000 characters). No fuss, no smart logic.

Lines 

Cut every string at the length limit, but if the cut intersects a line, the entire line is moved to the next page. If the line itself is over the length limit, it's split as per Words.

Words 

Cut every string at the length limit, but if the cut intersects a word (one or more characters surrounded by whitespace or the start/end of the string), the entire word is moved to the next page. If the word itself is over the length limit, it's split as per Verbatim.

enum DSharpPlus.Interactivity.TimeoutBehaviour : byte
strong
Enumerator
Ignore 

Doesn't do anything when the time runs out.

DeleteReactions 

Deletes the reactions on the message when the time runs out (this includes non-pagination reactions)

DeleteMessage 

Deletes the entire message when the time runs out (does not include the caller message)

Ignore 

Interactivity ignores message after timeout. No actions are performed

DeleteReactions 

Interactivity removes all reactions after timeout

DeleteMessage 

Interactivity deletes the message after timeout
